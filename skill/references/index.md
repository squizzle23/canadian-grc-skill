## Known gaps in the library (may need external verification)

- **CSA Z246.1** — copyrighted standard published by CSA Group; not freely distributable. AER Reg 84 work requires this for specific section citations. Purchase/license via `https://www.csagroup.org`.
- **PIPA BC and PIPA AB full texts** — not included; orientation coverage only in `privacy-provincial.md`. Retrieve from BC Laws and Alberta King's Printer respectively.
- **Bill C-26 / CCSPA** — the statute is not included as it was not enacted at the time of this skill's publication. Verify current status and effective date from Parliament of Canada.
- **AIDA / Bill C-27** — not yet in force as of early 2026. Verify current status from Parliament of Canada and Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada (ISED).
- **PHIPA current consolidation** — PHIPA has been amended materially since 2004. For specific statutory language on post-2004 amendments, check Ontario e-Laws directly.
- **Loi 25 current consolidation** — summary reflects the statute current to December 11, 2025. Verify amendments after this date via LégisQuébec (`https://www.legisquebec.gouv.qc.ca`).

## How to use this index

1. **Start with the curated reference** for the regulator involved. It contains verified section numbers, principles, and output patterns.
2. **Escalate to the authoritative source** at the regulator's website only when you need verbatim language or a section not covered in the curated reference.
3. **Check this index's "Known gaps"** before citing anything not in the curated references — confirm externally or flag the gap to the user.
4. **Use `incident-playbook.md`** when any incident-reporting question comes up, regardless of regulator.
5. **Use `evidence-catalog.md`** when the organization is preparing for an examination or asks about readiness.
